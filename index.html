Meet the team

Vivienne

Studying Real Estate, International Business and Communication Vivienne brings bountiful knowledge in endless fields to Pride and Property that will be directly transferred to you, in helping you out of your situation. Vivienne grew up in a family business and understands both the complexity and importance of maintaining a solid family unit under all circumstances, good and bad. The soft touch of the company, Vivienne prides herself on her empathetic touch and unmatched listening skills.

Liam

Born and raised in Brisbane, but currently residing in Sydney, Liam has been working in the property and tourism industry for over 15 years. He has worked as Chief Operating Officer for tourism companies across Canada and the United States, a sales manager and operations manager for Australian and New Zealand tourism companies, a portfolio specialist and corporate social responsibility officer in Australia, and a cleaner in the UK. Aside from his work with P&P Liam believes deeply in giving back to community, lecturing part time in music, running drumming workshops with kids in hospital, a palliative care choir and variety shows with his students for residents at the local aged care facility. He loves being with P&P as it is a chance to be creative with solutions to strike a win-win situation and a privilege to walk with people in what can be a stressful and vulnerable time.

Ron

Ron is our reliable and all 'round top bloke builder. With over 35 years of building experience and project management, Ron brings expertise, feasibility, knowledge, and great know how to Pride and Property, so you can rest easy knowing that you're in good hands.
